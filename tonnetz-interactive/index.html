    How to use this interactive tonnetz diagram:
    • The notes are set to Sa = 220 Hz.
    • Use the slider at the bottom to transpose.
    • Click on a circle to play the note, or on a triangle to play the chord.
    • Shift+click for the octave above; Alt+Shift+click or Ctrl+Shift+click for the octave below.
    • Note: this page is not optimized for mobile devices.
